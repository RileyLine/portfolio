VOLUNTEER

There are tons of volunteer options!

Here is a short list of some of the volunteer opportunities we need to make the fair a success:

  • Gatekeepers/Greeters: provide information and directions to people at the arrival gates. Responsible for handling money with regards to collecting entrance fees.
  • Beer Garden Staff: Smart servers needed, ticket sales, clean up crews
  • Setup/Knockdown Crew: required several days before the Fair to prepare for the Fair and the Sunday/Monday after the Fair to cleanup and put it all away.
  • Booth Crew: required to help man the Port Hope Fair booth and provide information to visitors of the booth/Fair.
  • Event helpers: required to assist the Director-in-charge of events such as, the Horse Show; setting up jumps, giving out ribbons, etc.
  • Board Members: required to help plan next year’s fair. The Board meets the second Wednesday of each month at the Town Park Recreation Centre.

Fill out the form below to receive additional info about volunteer opportunities.

BURSARY

Port Hope and District Agricultural Society endeavours to promote agriculture presently and into the future. As such, the society wishes to make available an annual $500.00 bursary to an individual aged 21 years or under who is furthering their education in one of the following agriculture related curriculum undertaken at an accredited Canadian college or university;

  • B.Sc. Agriculture
  • Veterinary Medicine
  • Diploma in Agriculture

Monies will be paid out after successful completion of one year in any of the above programs. Applicants must be a resident of Northumberland County.
